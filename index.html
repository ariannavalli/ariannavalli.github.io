ABOUT ME

I come from the countryside near Perugia (Italy) and I’m an undergraduate student in Physics at the University of Trento.
In particular, I’ve recently become interested in Mathematical Physics, a field that lies at the intersection between Theoretical Physics and Applied Mathematics.
Beyond the “pure” aspects of these disciplines, I’m passionate about the history of Physics and Mathematics.

I’m also the public relations manager of the Trento committee of the Italian Association of Physics Students (AISF).
Additionally, I’m often active in initiatives to promote equity and diversity in STEM, especially about women in STEM.
Since 2026, I'm writer and editor for Forbes Next Leaders and Sistemi di Riferimento.
